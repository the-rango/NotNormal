About the game

Embark on an epic adventure at UCI!

UCI is in peril! With the unsuspected threat lurking in the dark, it is now up to YOU, an inexperienced first-year undergrad, to save this university and all who live in it while also saving your grades. Ximi and the Legend of the Grade Saver is a 2D role-playing game that lets the players live like Ximi, and explore and learn about UCI.
